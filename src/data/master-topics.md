- **Pillar 4 (Ontology):** Comprehensive glossary of phonetic terms; IPA charts for Portuguese; dialect comparison matrices; pronunciation variation databases; speaker accent classification taxonomy.

- **Pillar 6 (Tools):** Interactive IPA chart with audio samples; pronunciation comparison tool (European vs. Brazilian); stress pattern analyzer; minimal pair practice generator; voice recording comparison tool.
- **Pillar 7 (Challenges):** Common pronunciation mistakes by English speakers; mastering nasal vowels; understanding reduced vowels in unstressed positions; regional accent comprehension; strategies for improving spoken fluency.
- **Sources:** Wikipedia - Portuguese phonology; Ethnologue - Portuguese language data; Portuguese Language Museum (São Paulo); Instituto Camões phonetic resources; University of Lisbon phonology research.
